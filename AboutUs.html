Behind HealthConnect

At HealthConnect, our journey began with a vision to make healthcare accessible for every individual in Metro Manila. Founded in 2024, we recognized the urgent need for a platform that connects people with free or low-cost healthcare services. Our mission is to bridge the gap between patients and healthcare resources, ensuring that everyone can easily find the care they need.







Our Core Values

Accessibility


We believe that healthcare should be available to everyone, regardless of their circumstances or background.

Collaboration


We work hand-in-hand with healthcare providers, NGOs, and the community to create a robust network of support.

Empowerment


We empower individuals with the knowledge and resources they need to make informed health decisions.

Integrity


We operate with transparency and honesty, ensuring that our services are reliable and trustworthy.

Compassion


We approach our mission with empathy, understanding the challenges individuals face in accessing healthcare.



Mission

To empower every Filipino with seamless access to free and low-cost healthcare services through a centralized platform, breaking down barriers to health.

Vision

A healthier Metro Manila where everyone can easily access the healthcare they need, fostering a community of well-being and equity.




Goals


We champion the belief that healthcare should be a right, not a privilege, ensuring it is available to everyone.

We forge strong partnerships with healthcare providers, NGOs, and the community, creating a powerful network of support.

We equip individuals with the knowledge and resources they need to make informed health decisions, fostering self-advocacy.

We operate with transparency and honesty, building trust through reliable and consistent services.

We approach our mission with empathy, understanding the unique challenges individuals face in navigating the healthcare landscape.
