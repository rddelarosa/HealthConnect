Frequently Asked Questions (FAQs)

Got Questions? We’ve Got Answers!


Find answers to common queries related to our services and healthcare in general. Our FAQs are categorized for your convenience, making it easy to find the information you need.



Consultations (FAQs)

What types of consultations are available?

We offer general medical, specialist, mental health, and diagnostic consultations, depending on the availability of services in partnered hospitals and clinics.

How do I book a consultation?

Simply log in to your account, choose a healthcare provider from the list, and follow the step-by-step instructions to select a consultation type, date, and time.

Are online consultations available?

Yes, online consultations are available through video calls with healthcare providers for specific services. Look for providers offering this option when booking.



Appointments (FAQs)

How can I schedule an appointment?

Log in in to the website, go to the "Appointments" section, select your preferred provider and service, and choose a convenient date and time.

What should I bring to my appointment?

Bring any valid ID, medical records (if applicable), and any required documents specified in your appointment confirmation.

Can I change or cancel my appointment?

Yes, you can modify or cancel your appointment through your account dashboard. Please ensure changes are made at least 24 hours in advance.
