HealthConnect

Your Guide to Free Healthcare in Metro Manila

Manila HealthConnect is a free online platform dedicated to connecting Manileños with hospitals, clinics, and NGOs that offer no-cost or low-cost healthcare services. Our mission is to bridge the gap between patients and essential healthcare resources, making it easier for everyone to access consultations, diagnostics, mental health support, and more—all from the comfort of home.

Explore our services and start your journey to better health today!

Our Story


At HealthConnect, our journey began with a simple vision: to make healthcare accessible for every Juan.

Founded in 2024, we recognized the challenges many individuals face in finding free or low-cost medical services amidst crowded hospitals and limited information. Our platform was created to bridge this gap by connecting users with hospitals, clinics, and NGOs dedicated to providing essential healthcare.

Join us in this mission to promote healthier communities!
